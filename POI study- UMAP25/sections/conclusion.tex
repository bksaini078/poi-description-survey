\section{Conclusion}


This study demonstrates that Large Language Models can effectively personalize POI descriptions to improve user engagement and information quality. A study of 51 participants and 510 comparisons across 10 POIs found that AI-generated adaptations significantly improved clarity ($p = 0.0218$) and showed marginal gains in trustworthiness ($p = 0.050$). The results suggest that personalization helps users form more confident assessments by highlighting details that match their interests, with participants reporting high comfort using AI-generated content (mean = 3.94/5). These findings show that travel platforms can use LLMs to scale personalized content without the high costs of manual writing. Ultimately, this research highlights that adapting how content is described is just as important as the recommendation itself for creating a relevant and engaging user experience.
